Active

Niacinamide

INCI: Niacinamide

Water-soluble vitamin B3 derivative. Inhibits melanosome transfer, reduces sebum excretion, reinforces the ceramide barrier. Optimal range: 2–5% w/w, pH 5–7.

Brightening Barrier support

Humectant

Hyaluronic Acid

INCI: Sodium Hyaluronate

High-MW (1500 kDa) stays on surface; low-MW (10–50 kDa) penetrates deeper. Holds up to 1,000× its weight in water. Film-forming at higher concentrations.

Hydration Film-forming

Antioxidant

Ascorbic Acid

INCI: Ascorbic Acid

L-ascorbic acid is the bioactive form of vitamin C. Collagen synthesis cofactor, tyrosinase inhibitor, radical scavenger. Highly unstable — pH must stay below 3.5 for stability.

pH sensitive Brightening
